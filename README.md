# ⚡ Electric Vehicle Analytics – Washington State

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the **growth and distribution of Electric Vehicles (EVs) in Washington State**.  
It focuses on identifying trends in EV adoption, regional distribution, and manufacturer performance to support data-driven decision-making for sustainable transportation.

---

## 🌍 Introduction
The increasing demand for sustainable transportation has accelerated the shift from traditional fuel-based vehicles to electric vehicles.  
Washington State is a leader in EV adoption due to strong environmental policies, renewable energy usage, and initiatives like:
- Clean Energy Transformation Act (CETA)  
- Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) mandate  

The state aims to make all new passenger vehicles **zero-emission by 2030**.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
There is limited consolidated analysis of EV adoption across:
- Different years  
- Counties and cities  
- Manufacturers and models  

Understanding these patterns is essential for:
- Policy planning  
- Infrastructure development (charging stations)  
- Future energy strategies  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total EV registrations in Washington State  
- Study year-wise growth trends  
- Examine county-wise and city-wise distribution  
- Compare EV brands and models  
- Analyze electric range trends by model year  
- Generate insights for future planning  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Rapid growth in EV adoption over recent years  
- Significant variation across counties and cities  
- Certain brands dominate the EV market  
- Continuous improvement in EV range due to technological advancements  

---

## 🧠 Conclusion
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of EV adoption trends in Washington State.  
It highlights how EV usage has evolved over time and identifies key patterns in regional distribution and manufacturer preferences.  

The insights derived can support policymakers, businesses, and researchers in:
- Expanding EV infrastructure  
- Promoting sustainable mobility  
- Planning future energy solutions
